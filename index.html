1.After extensive code refactoring and development, the Dancing Line Fanmade Website is finally rolling out its full major update. We took inspiration from the official DLRS fan community website, reworked it, and integrated our own ideas and core vision.
2.The official Dancing Line typeface is now applied across the entire site, delivering an experience on par with the original game!
3.All in-game level cards have been relocated to the [Games] page, with a dedicated standalone site developed for each level collection. We have also added a download feature: simply click to download and play select levels, and we welcome you to give it a try!
4.We will continue to roll out ongoing updates for fanmade levels and development resources. Stay tuned for more!
5.This is a non-commercial fan-run website. Every revision is crafted with the immense dedication and hard work of our development team, and we would like to extend our sincerest gratitude to all of our supporters.
6.A brand-new theme feature has been added, now accessible via the sidebar. New themes will be added on an irregular basis, and 1 to 3 exclusive themes will be released randomly for every major holiday!
7.You are more than welcome to submit and host your own level collections on this website!
8. The Qingming theme is now fully available for both PC and mobile. However, it is recommended not to switch themes frequently, as this may cause lag. To resolve this, simply refresh the webpage (or exit and re-enter the client on mobile).
9.The Dancing Line Fan-Made Website client is now live and available on the download page. Simply click the card to download it. We guarantee that the client is completely safe and free from any malicious risks, so feel free to download it.
10.The download feature is now fully launched and available on certain cards, with plans to expand it to various developers in the future.
