公告

中文版：
1. 经过好久的代码重构和编写，现在跳舞的线饭制网终于迎来全面大更新，我们模仿了DLRS同人群官网，但是我们加以改造，并且融入了我们的想法与立场
2. 现在全局使用了跳舞的线官方字体，带给你媲美原版的体验！
3. 所有游戏关卡卡片已迁移至【游戏】页面，并且为每个合集单独开发了相应的网站，我们还接入了下载功能，点击即可下载体验部分关卡，欢迎前往体验
4. 后续将持续更新饭制关卡与开发资源，敬请关注！
5.本网站属于非营业性的同人网站，每次编辑都充满了我们开发者的心血与努力，非常感谢支持我们的全体成员
6.新增主题功能，现在在侧边栏即可找到，里面会不定时更新新的主题，每次重大节假日会随机更新1～3个专属主题！
7.欢迎上架你们的合集到此网站！
8.现在全面上线清明主题电脑和手机都有适配，但这里建议不要频繁切换主题，否则会出现卡顿现象，解决方法是刷新网页（客户端退出重进）
9.跳舞的线饭制网客户端上线，已经上架到了下载页面，点击卡片下载即可，我们承诺客户端没有任何恶意风险，请放心下载
10.下载功能全新上线，现在已上架到部分卡片，未来将扩大至各开发者
11.增加收藏功能，快来收藏你喜欢的合集吧！
English Version
1.After a long period of code refactoring and development, the Dancing Line Fan-made Website has finally received a comprehensive major update. We drew inspiration from the DLRS fan community official website, but we have transformed it and integrated our own ideas and perspectives.
2.The official Dancing Line font is now used globally, bringing you an experience comparable to the original game!
3.All game level cards have been moved to the Games page, and we have developed a dedicated sub-site for each collection. We have also added a download feature: click to download and experience some levels. Welcome to check it out!
4.We will continue to update fan-made levels and development resources in the future. Stay tuned!
5.This website is a non-profit fan-made site. Every edit is filled with the hard work and dedication of our developers. Thank you so much to all our supporters!
6.A new theme feature has been added, which you can find in the sidebar. New themes will be updated irregularly, and 1 to 3 exclusive themes will be randomly released for every major holiday!
7.You are welcome to submit your own collections to this website!
8.The Qingming Festival theme is now fully available and optimized for both desktop and mobile devices. However, we advise against switching themes too frequently, as it may cause lagging. The solution is to refresh the webpage (or restart the client if you are using it)
. 9.The Dancing Line Fan-made Website client is now live and available on the Download page. Just click the card to download. We promise the client contains no malicious software and is completely safe to use.
10.The brand-new download feature has been launched and is currently available on some cards. We will expand it to all developers in the future.
11.A favorites feature has been added! Come and save your favorite collections
